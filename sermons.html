Finding Peace in Chaos

Pastor John Davis • March 26, 2025 • Series: Unshakeable Faith

Download Share Audio Only Notes

In this powerful message, Pastor John explores how we can find peace even in life's most chaotic moments. Drawing from Philippians 4, he shares practical steps to maintain spiritual equilibrium when everything around us seems to be falling apart.

Key Scripture

"Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God. And the peace of God, which transcends all understanding, will guard your hearts and your minds in Christ Jesus." — Philippians 4:6-7
