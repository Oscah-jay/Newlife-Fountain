Local Outreach

Making a difference in our own backyard

Homeless Support Network

Our Homeless Support Network provides meals, shelter, counseling, and job training to individuals experiencing homelessness in our city. Through partnerships with local businesses and government agencies, we're creating pathways to stable housing and employment.

  • Served over 5,000 meals in the past year
  • Provided temporary shelter to 200+ individuals
  • Helped 230 people secure permanent housing
  • Connected 180 individuals with stable employment
50 program participants report improved quality of life
Support This Project

Food & Resource Center

Our Food & Resource Center provides nutritious food, household essentials, and support services to families facing food insecurity. Beyond meeting immediate needs, we connect families with resources for long-term stability.

  • Distributing 5,000+ pounds of food monthly
  • Serving 50+ families each week
  • Offering nutrition education and cooking classes
  • Providing referrals to additional community resources
35 increase in families served since last year

Global Missions

Extending our reach around the world

Education Centers in East Africa

We're building and supporting education centers in underserved communities across East Africa, providing quality education, nutritious meals, and healthcare to children who would otherwise lack access to these essentials.

  • 5 education centers established across 3 countries
  • Over 600 students receiving education
  • Teacher training programs improving educational quality
  • Community involvement ensuring sustainability
30 students continue to secondary education
