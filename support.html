Frequently Asked Questions

What is a tithe?

+

A tithe is giving the first 10% of your income back to God through the local church. It's an act of worship and obedience that acknowledges God as the provider of all we have.

How is my giving used?

+

Your giving supports our church's ministries, outreach programs, missions, facilities, and staff who work to fulfill our mission of reaching people and building lives.

Is my giving tax-deductible?

+

No, all donations to our church are not tax-deductible. Your donations and givings tailored are tailored towards supporting the ministry's activities and outreach programs hence are not tax-deductible but when sending through mobile transfers the relevant charges are applied
