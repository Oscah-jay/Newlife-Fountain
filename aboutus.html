A Home of Faith, Love, and Purpose

Welcome to Newlife Fountain Ministries, a place where faith comes alive, love overflows, and hope is renewed! Here, every heart finds a home, every soul discovers purpose, and every journey is embraced with grace. Join us as we worship, grow, and serve together in the light of God's boundless love!

Who We Are

At Newlife Fountain Ministries, we are more than just a congregation—we are a family united by faith, love, and a shared mission to shine God's light in the world. Rooted in the Word and led by the Spirit, we are a community where lives are transformed, hearts are healed, and purpose is discovered. Whether you are seeking a deeper relationship with God, a place to belong, or a mission to serve, you'll find a warm welcome and a home in His presence. Join us as we grow together in faith and impact the world with Christ's love!
