About Us

Welcome to our ministry!
We are a community of believers dedicated to serving and glorifying God.

We aim to spread the love and teachings of Jesus Christ, reaching out to those in need and welcoming all into our family.

At our ministry, you'll find a warm and welcoming environment where you can grow in your faith, connect with others, and make a difference in the world.

Our Values

  • Faith: We believe in the power of faith to transform lives and bring hope to the world.
  • Love: We strive to love God with all our heart, soul, and mind, and to love our neighbors as ourselves.
  • Community: We are committed to building a strong and supportive community where everyone is valued and welcomed.
  • Service: We are called to serve others with compassion and generosity, following the example of Jesus Christ.

OUR MISSION
To serve God by spreading the Gospel and serving others by acts of kindness and empathy, equipping them with lifeskills and spiritual nurturing

OUR VISION:
To spread the good news to the community and beyond and equip believers into kingdom Service
